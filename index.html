The project

A ten-week applied ethnographic research project examining how imambargahs in Quetta's mixed-population urban areas materialise the forced absence of the Hazara community. Part of the Summer Tehqiq Research Programme 2026 at Habib University.

A note on location data

Given the ongoing security situation faced by the Hazara community in Quetta, this project does not publish the specific locations of documented sites. All spatial data is held securely by the research team.

Ethics

No photographs of individuals without explicit verbal consent. No personally identifiable data collected. Compliant with Habib University research ethics guidelines.
